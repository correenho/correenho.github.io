This is about Correen Xilin He.


Correen was born and raised in Guangzhou, a southern metropolis of China. She obtained a BA in English (Journalism and Media) from GDUFS. Then she went to England for postgraduate studies and achieved an MSc in International Business from Nottingham University Business School. After that, she returned to China to work for Procter and Gamble in Consumer and Market Knowledge for 12 years.


Now Correen lives in Irvine, California with her husband Alex and daughter Emily.
